The Chafetz Chayim

Leader of Orthodox Jewry in the early Twentieth Century

Rabbi Yisrael Meir Kagan, known as "the Chafetz Chayim"

Rabbi Yisrael Meir Kagan, known as "the Chafetz Chayim" (by the name of his most famous book), was the one of the greatest Rabbis of the twentieth century.

He wrote many important works on Jewish law, including:

  • Chafetz Chayim - the laws of improper speech
  • Mishnah Berurah - The laws of regular days and special days (6 volumes)
  • Ahavas Chesed - The laws of performing kindness to others

He authored many other works, on various subjects.
